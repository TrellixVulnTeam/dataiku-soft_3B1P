You can access different views of the flow here.

  • The Default view lets you see all datasets and recipes of your flow, with objects colored by type.
  • The Tags view lets you see elements of your flow based on their tags. When using this view you can quickly tags elements by right clicking them.
  • The Recipe engines view lets you check which engine is used by each of the recipes of your flow.
  • The Check consistency tool controls that the schemas of the input and outputs of a visual recipe are consistent.

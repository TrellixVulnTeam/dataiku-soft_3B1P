Once you have made some steps to process your data, you can:

  • Use the charts to visualize your transformed data
  • Create Machine Learning models based on your transformed data

You may also want to deploy your preparation script as a new recipe in the Flow.
This creates a new dataset holding your transformed data. You can then use it as input for other transformations

To learn more:

  • Our tutorials Basics 102 and 103 will guide you through the concepts of deploying recipes to the Flow and using the lab,
  • Our Machine learning basic courses teach you how to make your first Machine Learning model

You are currently seeing a preview of the impact of your step.

DSS shows how much your step modifies your data, and every change made is highlighted:

  • Modified data appears in blue
  • Deleted rows appear in strike-through
  • ...

If you are satisfied with your changes, you can disable preview.

At any time, you can click the eye icon of a step to view the impact of this step. Subsequent steps will be temporarily disabled.

Disabling preview is not mandatory, you can keep adding steps, and DSS will always show the impact of the last one

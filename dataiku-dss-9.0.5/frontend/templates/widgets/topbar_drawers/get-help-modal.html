We're sorry that you're experiencing trouble or are having difficulties achieving your goals with DSS. Here are some ways you can get help.

Contact your DSS administrators or company support resources

Your DSS administrators can provide initial help, analysis of issue, and will contact Dataiku Support if needed.

Ask our online resources

Ask questions, get help, share best practices and interact with other Dataiku users on the Community.

Check our online resources

Our Learn section contains a wealth of information about Dataiku, howtos, tutorials, documentation, …

Chat with Dataiku

Start a live chat conversation with Dataiku. Please note that this support channel is provided on a purely best-effort basis.

Open a support ticket

We strongly recommend that you get in touch with your DSS administrators before opening a support ticket. Dataiku does not have access to your DSS instances.
Please note that support is provided to Free Edition user on a purely best-effort basis.
